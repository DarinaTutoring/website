My approach to tutoring

I offer a free 15–20min meeting to meet with parents and students who are interested, and a half price first lesson, to see whether you think I am a good fit for your needs - please get in touch with a suggested time if you would like to e-meet.

Unless you have a specific target area or list from a teacher, the first few lessons are spent identifying current working level, and areas of weakness or interest. Then, we build a plan to get where we want to be.

I have an experience with a variety of students, from stretch and challenge lessons to foundation GCSE support, working with most mainstream exam boards (AQA, Edexcel, OCR).

Through my tutoring I hope to encourage students, building up their confidence as well as giving notes and advice on how to hit mark schemes. My lessons plans wholly adapt to the needs of the student, but a typical lesson focusses on recapping content that we have identified as a weak area, then testing their understanding with buildable difficulty questions.
